Tonny Tonny Chopper:

Tony Tony Chopper is a doctor and member of the Straw Hat Pirates in One Piece.

He is a reindeer who ate the Human-Human Fruit, giving him human intelligence and transformation abilities. He’s the crew’s doctor and specializes in medicine and anatomy.

Chopper can transform into multiple combat forms using his Rumble Balls. He was trained by Dr. Hiriluk and Dr. Kureha in Drum Island.

His dream is to become a doctor who can cure any disease. He is often mistaken for a pet because of his cute appearance.
